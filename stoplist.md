# Hybrid Pipe Organ – Stoplist v3.2
**3 Manuals + Pedal**

This revision incorporates:
- M5 (1′ Violin / Flachflute) moved into the Swell box as a high string color
- Choir division made entirely borrowed / digital (“playground manual”)
- No borrowing out of tremmed ranks
- W2 physically split into unenclosed (A) and Swell (B) chests
- W1 extended downward to form a true open 8′ flute rank

Legend  
(P) = real pipes  
(D) = digital  
(E) = extension / unification  
(B) = borrowed

## PHYSICAL SUMMARY (v3.2)

### Swell Box (expressive, tremmed)
- M4 — Salicional (61 pipes)
- M3 — Voix Celeste (49 pipes)
- M5 — High string / Piccolo (49 pipes - dedicated chest)
- W2 — Swell Gedackt / Flute (85 pipes)
- Digital Swell reeds & mixture (Speakers)
- Single tremulant

### Unenclosed
- M1 — Principal chorus (61 pipes)
- W1 — Open flute 8′ / 4′ (85 pipes + 12 to-be-constructed on floor)
- W2-B — Bourdon family low (12 on floor)
- W3 — Tibia bass (24 large pipes)
- R1 — Free-reed subbass (no pipes, just a box)
- Digital pedal foundations

### Choir
- No physical pipes
- Borrowed and digital only

---

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES ENFORCED
- No borrowing out of tremmed ranks
- No rank serves contradictory wind behaviors
- Manual 16′ ≠ Pedal 16′
- Digital ranks fill brilliance and extreme ranges, not foundations
